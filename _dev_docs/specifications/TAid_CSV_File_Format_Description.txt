Format of .csv files for TAid

*****
Enrolment lists - Indicate what lecture, tutorial, and (if applicable) 
what practical section each student is enrolled in.

Format:

utorID,lecture_section,tutorial_section,practical_section

Example:

canvi241,L0003,T0002,
racrob58,L0003,T0004,
couade63,L0003,T0001,
staild32,L0003,T0003,
bisbea71,L0003,T0002,
petbil69,L0003,T0002,
.
.
.


*****
Student lists - Standard list of students for a class. The format allows
for utorID, student number, and 3 additional ids (id1-id3) for each
student. 

Format:

utorID,last_name,first_name,student_number,email,id1,id2,id3

Example:

canvi241,Cano,Vi,209415323,Vi.Cano@nowhere.com,ci1nva24,vi2c1na4,24vic1an
racrob58,Racey,Robbin,206271143,Robbin.Racey@nowhere.com,8bor5rac,bcar58ro,rca5o8br
couade63,Couchman,Adella,2081414346,Adella.Couchman@nowhere.com,ued3oca6,d63ecuao,3uac6doe
staild32,Staplins,Ilda,2021649638,Ilda.Staplins@nowhere.com,a2tdlsi3,d2tsi3la,2iads3lt
bisbea71,Bison,Beatris,203143295,Beatris.Bison@nowhere.com,7sabibe1,eibs17ab,ie1asbb7
petbil69,Petaccio,Billy,2022239535,Billy.Petaccio@nowhere.com,e69tlbip,6bteli9p,tbeil69p
.
.
.

*****

Marks files: Standard MarkUs format, First 2 lines are the header

Header format:
"", item1_name, item2_name, ... , itemN_name
"", item1_value, item2_value, ... , itemN_value
 
The format of each row for the rest of the file is:

utorID, item1_mark, item2_mark, ... ,itemN_mark
.
.
.
 
Note: it is expected that any row may be missing one or more marks

Example:

"",Assignment_001,Assignment_002,Assignment_003,Assignment_004,Assignment_005
"",65,94,95,37,108
canvi241,54,58,,18,51
racrob58,56,61,95,18,72
couade63,55,59,75,19,67
staild32,55,59,31,20,44
bisbea71,55,58,85,18,64
.
.
.
